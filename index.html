F.A.Qs

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does your school only admit Muslim pupils?

    No! We admit pupils of all religions despite the fact that we have an Islamic background.

  • Do you teach following the standard curriculum?

    Yes! We teach both a secular and theological curriculam.

  • Don't you discriminate pupils of other religions?

    Not at all! We respect pupils of different doctrines and religions and even give them chance to worship their own God.

  • Do you have the boarding section for children who come from far?

    We have well facilitated dormitories taken care of by well trained professional matrons and wardens.

  • Will my child's health be catered for?

    We have a sick bay and a qualified school nurse to take care of sick pupils.

  • My my child's talent be developed?

    Yes! We give time to our pupils to participate in co-curricular activities which enable them to develop and discover their talents.
